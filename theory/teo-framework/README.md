# TEO Framework

The TEO framework represents a formal mathematical instantiation of the existing axioms, offering a structured and rigorous approach to understanding and implementing concepts in the realm of intelligence and systems. Within this framework, the abstract notion of "love" is operationalized through the formulation of constraint-based safety.

## Key Principles

1. **Formal Mathematical Instantiation**: The TEO framework translates abstract axioms into concrete mathematical representations, enabling systematic exploration and application.
2. **Operationalizing Love**: Love, often viewed as an abstract and subjective emotion, is framed within the TEO framework as a set of constraints that guide behavior and decision-making to ensure safety in various systems. This approach facilitates the integration of emotional intelligence into the operational parameters of intelligent systems.
