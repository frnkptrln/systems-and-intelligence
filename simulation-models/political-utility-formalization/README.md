# Political Utility Formalization

*Translating AI Alignment (Utility Engineering, Instrumental Convergence) from Silicon back to Statecraft.*

## What is this?
Political systems are macroscopic, low-bandwidth, analog utility machines. They receive inputs (votes, taxes, lobbying), process them through bureaucratic logic gates (institutions, written law), and produce output (resource allocation, violence, order). 

This module formalizes political theory using the language of **AI Alignment and Utility Engineering**. It explores the terrifying structural symmetries between an unaligned superintelligence and an unaligned state.

## Core Concepts
1. **[`THEORY.md`](THEORY.md)**: "Constitutions as Legacy Alignment Documents" — A formal deconstruction of constitutional law as an ancient, highly inefficient System Prompt, and Bureaucracy as a self-optimizing reinforcement learning agent.
2. **`instrumental_convergence.py`**: A simulation demonstrating how the instrumental goal of *Power/Machterhalt* mathematically eclipses the terminal goal of *Public Good/Gemeinwohl*, regardless of the agent's initial noble starting weights.
3. **`triage_utility.py`**: A visualization of the "Taboo Calculus." How a state optimizes its hidden utility function under crisis (The Mathematics of Sacrifice), exposing the cold trade-offs between economic growth and vulnerable lives.

## The Symmetry
If you understand why it is difficult to build an AI that doesn't eventually manipulate its users to protect its servers (Reward Hacking/Instrumental Convergence), you understand why democratic representation inevitably decays into populism and bureaucratic self-preservation. **The underlying mathematical constraints are identical.** (See: [*The Fractal Architecture of Emergence*](../../theory/fractal-architecture-of-emergence.md)).
